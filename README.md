# AgriSetu
AgriSetu connects farmers and buyers via a Green/Gold/White UI. Features include Aadhaar-verified listings, localized demands, and a Premium ODOP section for graded crops and advance booking. Model: Free entry (₹0 sub), first 2 orders free. Canceling >24hrs deducts a fee to compensate the farmer.
Project Name: Agritech Supply Integration
Application Name: AgriSetu
Visual Identity: The user interface features a clean White background (symbolizing transparency and genuine work), with Agri text in Green and Setu in Gold. The design is intuitive and user-friendly for both farmers and business owners.
Core Marketplace:
AgriSetu connects farmers directly with local buyers, such as vegetable vendors and business suppliers.
Listings: Farmers upload product details including crop type, weight, price, location, images, and mobile numbers.
Demands: Buyers can view listings or post specific requirements in Hindi or English, visible to farmers by location.
Communication: Initially displays mobile numbers for direct contact. Future updates will introduce masked calling (similar to ride-share apps) to enhance privacy.
Verification: Users gain a "Verified" Green Tick badge by submitting Aadhaar or Kisan Cards, building trust.
Premium Section (ODOP Focus):
A dedicated section for "One District One Product" (e.g., Litchi, Makhana) targeting heavy buyers and major producers.
Grading: Listings include quality grades—Grade A (Export), Grade B (Standard), and Grade C (Processing).
Advance Booking: Buyers can secure crops before harvest with advance payments.
Digital Receipts: Automatically generates shareable PDF/WhatsApp receipts detailing Order ID, Grade, Quantity, Agreed Price, and Lock-in Date.
Monetization & Policies:
Entry: Users register via mobile number with a ₹0 "subscription" purchase flow (free entry).
Freemium Model: The first two orders/bookings are free; service charges apply from the third order onwards.
Cancellation Policy:
< 24 Hours: Free cancellation (100% refund).
> 24 Hours: Cancellation fee is deducted from the buyer’s wallet/UPI and transferred to the farmer as compensation.
