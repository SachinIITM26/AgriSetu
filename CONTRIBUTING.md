# Contribution Guidelines

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the AgriSetu project!

## How to Contribute
1. **Fork the repository**: Click on the "Fork" button on the top right of the page to create your copy of the repository.
2. **Make your changes**: Clone your fork to your local machine and make the necessary changes.
3. **Commit your changes**: Use clear commit messages that describe your changes.
4. **Push to your fork**: Push your changes to your fork on GitHub.
5. **Open a Pull Request**: Once your changes are pushed, click the "New Pull Request" button on GitHub.

## Guidelines
- **Code Style**: Ensure your code follows the project's coding conventions.
- **Documentation**: Update documentation accordingly whenever you make changes to code.
- **Tests**: If applicable, include tests for your changes and ensure they pass.

## Contact
If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to the maintainers of the project.

Thank you for your contributions!
